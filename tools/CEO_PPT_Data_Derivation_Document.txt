  Eligibility is determined by 'DOJ' (Date of Joining) relative to the appraisal cycle:

  Logic:
    Rating 4 (Exceeds) : 'Rating' = '4'  — awarded to top performers

    Rating 2 (Below)   : 'Rating' = '2'  — below expectations

┌─ C5. Eligibility Logic Detail ────────────────────────────────────────────────

  The 'Apprisal' column stores computed eligibility. The underlying logic is:

  IF DOJ <= 1 April 2025         → 'Fully Eligible'     (full 12 months in cycle)
  IF DOJ between 1 Oct 2024
     and 31 March 2025           → 'Partially Eligible' (6–11 months in cycle)
  IF DOJ > 1 October 2025        → 'Not Eligible'       (< 6 months; MIS = joined after Oct 2025)

  Pro-rata increments: Partially Eligible employees receive a merit increment
  proportional to the number of months worked in the appraisal year.
